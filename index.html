Walking Into Math Class

Freshman year I chose a math class that was very challenging for me. I had dreaded walking into that class for months. It took a lot of courage for me to overcome my inferiority complex and go into the class with an open mind.

Moving to Boston

I was very nervous to move again overseas. I was older this time, and moving middle schools made me worried that people would already have friends and cliques. I was scared, but I also was ready to make new friends that I would get to keep for years.

The Big Race

My second regatta was at Massachusetts State Championships and my heart was in my mouth as we sat at the catch with our oars in the water. I waited for the official to say, "Attention...ROW!" and for us to begin the race. I was worried I was going to let my team down. Luckily, we crushed our race and won the gold.
